Frequently Asked Questions

To create your first deck, tap the "+" button on the My Decks screen or use the "Create Flashcard" button on the home screen. Give your deck a name, then start adding cards with questions and answers.
We offer Memory Match (find matching pairs by flipping cards) and Word Chef (arrange scrambled letters to form the correct answer). Both games make studying fun while reinforcing your learning.
The Pomodoro Timer helps you study in focused 25-minute sessions. Simply tap "Start" to begin your study session, and the timer will notify you when it's time for a break.

Your progress is tracked automatically. The home screen shows your study streak, and you can see remaining cards in each deck. Mark cards as correct or incorrect to track your learning progress.
Yes, all your flashcard decks, progress, and settings are automatically saved locally on your device. Your data persists between app sessions.
In study mode, tap on a card to flip it and see the answer. Mark it as "Correct" or "Incorrect" to help the app track which cards you need to review more often.
