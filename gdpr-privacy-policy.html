Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance

Data Controller

Flashcard Fun+ (app.rork.flashcard-funland)

This mobile application is designed to help users learn through flashcards and educational games.

What Data We Collect

Our app collects and processes the following personal data locally on your device:

Learning Content Data

  • Flashcard Decks: Custom deck names and flashcard content (front/back text) that you create
  • Study Materials: Any educational content you input into the app

Learning Progress Data

  • Study Statistics: Daily streak counters, cards reviewed per day, longest streak records
  • Learning States: Your progress on individual flashcards (correct/incorrect/unreviewed status)
  • Session Data: Last review dates and deck access timestamps
  • Performance Metrics: Total cards reviewed and learning consistency tracking

Technical Data

  • App Preferences: Theme settings, language preferences, and other app configurations
  • Device Storage: All data is stored locally using AsyncStorage on your device

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): To provide core app functionality and track your learning progress
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR): For optional features like analytics or personalized recommendations (if implemented)

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All your data is stored exclusively on your device using secure AsyncStorage
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit, store, or process your data on external servers
  • Device Security: Data security depends on your device's built-in security measures
  • No Third-Party Access: Your learning data is not shared with any third parties

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

Right of Access (Article 15)

You can view all your data within the app's interface at any time.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can edit, update, or correct your flashcards and learning data directly in the app.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You can delete individual flashcards, entire decks, or reset your learning progress within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Since data is stored locally, you can export your data through device backup systems (iCloud, Google Backup).

Right to Object (Article 21)

You can disable specific features or data collection within the app settings.

Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can limit how the app processes your data by adjusting app permissions and settings.

Data Retention

  • Active Use: Data is retained as long as the app is installed and you continue using it
  • Inactive Periods: No automatic deletion occurs during periods of non-use
  • App Removal: All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Manual Deletion: You can delete specific data at any time through the app interface

Data Sharing and Transfers

  • No External Sharing: We do not share your personal data with third parties
  • No International Transfers: All data remains on your local device
  • No Marketing Use: Your data is not used for marketing or advertising purposes

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. For users under 16:

  • Parental consent may be required under GDPR Article 8
  • The same data protection principles apply
  • No special categories of personal data are collected

Changes to This Policy

We may update this GDPR compliance information when:

  • App functionality changes significantly
  • New data processing activities are introduced
  • Legal requirements change

Contact Information

For GDPR-related inquiries, data subject requests, or privacy concerns:

  • App Support: Contact through your device's app store
  • Data Protection Officer: Not applicable (local storage only)

Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Technical Implementation

This app implements privacy by design:

  • Data Minimization: Only necessary data for app functionality is collected
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is used only for intended learning purposes
  • Storage Limitation: No unnecessary data retention
  • Integrity and Confidentiality: Secure local storage implementation
